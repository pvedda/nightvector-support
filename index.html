Clear skies start with a clear answer.

Help with sky orientation, location and motion access, Apple Weather, purchases, and the manual Telescope Finder.

Sky map orientation

Allow location while using the app so the map can match your horizon. Live orientation also needs Motion access and works best away from magnets, vehicles, power supplies, and metal structures. If the view drifts, move the phone in a figure eight and try again in a less magnetically noisy area.

Telescope Finder accuracy

NightVector provides manual Push-To guidance; it does not move or control your telescope. Use bright alignment stars above 20 degrees altitude, choose stars 40 to 120 degrees apart, and add the suggested third star. Accuracy depends on phone mount quality, sensors, magnetic interference, star choice, and eyepiece field of view.

Weather or location unavailable

Check iPhone Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > NightVector and allow access while using the app. Apple Weather also needs a network connection. Forecast visibility is practical weather guidance, not a dedicated astronomical seeing measurement.

Purchases and subscriptions

  • Use Restore Purchases in NightVector Settings after reinstalling or changing devices.
  • Manage or cancel a subscription from iPhone Settings > Apple Account > Subscriptions.
  • Purchases are billed and refunded by Apple. NightVector cannot access your payment method.

Still need help?

Include your iPhone model, iOS version, NightVector version, and the steps that caused the issue. Do not send precise observing locations or payment details.
