Privacy policy

Your observing data stays yours.

NightVector has no advertising, cross-app tracking, or third-party analytics. Version 1 does not operate a NightVector account system or backend.

Effective date: July 15, 2026

Location

With your permission, NightVector uses precise location to calculate the sky above you, rise and set times, and local observing conditions. When you request weather, coordinates are sent to Apple Weather to return a forecast. NightVector does not use location for tracking, advertising, or profiling and does not link it to a NightVector account.

Motion and orientation

Device motion is processed on your iPhone to orient the live sky map and provide manual Telescope Finder guidance. NightVector does not transmit or sell motion readings.

On-device information

Settings, favorites, telescope equipment, observing plans, and logs are stored on your device. NightVector does not receive them. They remain until you remove them in the app or delete the app and its data.

Purchases

Apple processes subscriptions and lifetime purchases through the App Store. NightVector reads verified entitlement status from StoreKit to unlock features but does not receive your payment card details. Apple's privacy policy governs App Store purchase processing.

Apple Weather

Forecast requests and attribution are provided by Apple Weather. Apple's terms and privacy policy apply to information processed by that service. NightVector caches forecast results temporarily to reduce requests and improve reliability.

Tracking, analytics, and disclosure

NightVector does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs, does not track you across apps or websites, and does not sell personal information. Information may be disclosed only when required by law or when you choose to send it in a support request.

Policy changes

If NightVector's data practices change, this page and the App Store privacy disclosures will be updated before the changed behavior is released. The effective date above identifies the current policy.

Privacy questions

Contact the NightVector support channel. Do not include precise location or payment details unless they are essential to your question.
